Weather Py Analysis

From the analysis, the following conclusions can be drawn:

Temperature seems to have a clear correlation with latitude

Temperature increases as we approach the equator. 
However, temperature peaks at around 20 degrees latitude, not exactly at the equatorial line.
 
There is no strong relationship between latitude and cloudiness. 
Cloudiness and humidity do not show a strong correlation to latitude. 



There is no strong relationship between latitude and wind speed.
